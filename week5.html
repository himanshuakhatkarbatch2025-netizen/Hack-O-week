This travel log contains three memorable journeys. Use the navigation bar to explore each story.

Trip 1 – Mountain Adventure

Destination: Manali

Highlights: Snow trekking, river rafting

Memorable Experience: Watching sunrise from the mountain peak.

Challenge: Cold weather and steep climbs.

Lesson Learned: Preparation makes adventure safer and more fun.

Trip 2 – Beach Escape

Destination: Goa

Highlights: Beaches, seafood, nightlife

Memorable Experience: Sunset cruise with live music.

Challenge: Sunburn from staying too long at the beach.

Lesson Learned: Always carry sunscreen.

Trip 3 – Cultural Journey

Destination: Jaipur

Highlights: Forts, palaces, local markets

Memorable Experience: Visiting Amer Fort.

Challenge: Navigating crowded streets.

Lesson Learned: Local guides can enhance the experience.
